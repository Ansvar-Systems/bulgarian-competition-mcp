# Data Coverage — Bulgarian Competition MCP

## Source Authority

**CPC Bulgaria** — Commission for Protection of Competition (Комисия за защита на конкуренцията)
- Website: https://www.cpc.bg/
- Jurisdiction: Bulgaria (BG)
- Legal basis: ZZK — Zakon za Zashtita na Konkurentsiyata (Law on Protection of Competition)

---

## Enforcement Decisions

| Category | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Abuse of dominance | Decisions under ZZK Chapter VI — dominant position and monopoly conduct |
| Cartel enforcement | Decisions under ZZK Chapter V — prohibited agreements and concerted practices |
| Sector inquiries | Market investigations and sector-wide enforcement actions |
| Fines | Financial penalties for ZZK infringements |

**Case number format:** `КЗК-NNNN/YYYY` (Bulgarian) or `CPC-YYYY-NNN` (English transliteration)

---

## Merger Control

| Category | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Phase I clearance | Concentrations cleared without conditions |
| Phase II decisions | In-depth investigations including prohibited and conditional clearances |
| Concentrations | Acquisition of control, mergers, joint ventures under ZZK Chapter VII |

**Case number format:** `КЗК-К-NNN/YYYY`

## Coverage Gaps and Limitations

- Coverage is **periodic** — the database may lag official CPC publications by days to weeks.
- Historical decisions prior to digitization may be incomplete.
- Full Bulgarian-language text is indexed where available; English summaries may be partial.
- This is a **research tool** — verify all findings against primary CPC sources before relying on them professionally.

See `bg_comp_check_data_freshness` for current index statistics.
See `bg_comp_list_sources` for provenance metadata.
